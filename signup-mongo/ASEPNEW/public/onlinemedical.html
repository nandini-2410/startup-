Online Medical Center

Startup Cost: ₹2,00,000 - ₹8,00,000
An Online Medical Center provides virtual consultations, prescriptions, and follow-ups with licensed doctors and specialists. This model is convenient for patients and reduces overhead costs for providers. It requires a secure platform, certified professionals, and compliance with medical data privacy laws, making it a scalable and impactful health startup. Online medical startups, also known as healthtech startups, leverage technology to improve healthcare delivery and patient outcomes. They encompass various sectors, including telemedicine, digital health monitoring, electronic health records (EHR), and AI-driven diagnostics. These startups often aim to make healthcare more accessible, personalized, and proactive.
