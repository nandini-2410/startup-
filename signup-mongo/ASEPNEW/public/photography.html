Photography

Startup Cost: ₹1,50,000 - ₹6,00,000
A Photography startup offers creative visual content services for events, businesses, individuals, and social media. With the rise of digital platforms, demand for high-quality photos is growing in weddings, e-commerce, modeling, food, and product branding. You can specialize in:
  • Wedding and event photography
  • Portraits and portfolio shoots
  • Product photography for businesses
  • Nature, travel, and lifestyle content for social media
Startup essentials include a good DSLR/mirrorless camera, lighting equipment, editing software (like Adobe Lightroom), and a portfolio website or Instagram page. Initially, you can offer discounted shoots to build
