Dietitian Services

Startup Cost: ₹80,000 - ₹2,50,000
Starting a dietitian services business is a rewarding opportunity to help people lead healthier lives through personalized nutrition planning. You can operate independently, collaborate with clinics, or build an online presence offering consultations via video calls.

A registered dietitian can cater to various groups such as diabetic patients, athletes, pregnant women, or those seeking weight loss. Your offerings can include:
  • Personalized meal and fitness plans
  • Nutrition workshops and webinars
  • Diet coaching and follow-up programs
  • Subscription-based diet monitoring apps or services
Essential requirements include a certification in dietetics or clinical nutrition, legal registration, and possibly a license to practice. The business can be home-based or operated from a small clinic space. Marketing through Instagram, YouTube, or health blogs is very effective. Partnerships with gyms, yoga studios, or hospitals can boost credibility and reach. The growing awareness about fitness and preventive healthcare makes this a promising and impactful business idea.
