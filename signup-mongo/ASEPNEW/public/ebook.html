E-book Writing

Startup Cost: ₹20,000 - ₹1,00,000
E-book Writing is a creative and educational startup opportunity where you author and publish digital books on topics such as academics, personal development, fiction, tutorials, or industry knowledge. These books can be sold or offered as lead magnets on platforms like Amazon Kindle, Google Books, or your own website. You can start by:
  • Identifying a niche like education, exam prep, or storytelling
  • Writing and formatting your book using tools like MS Word or Canva
  • Publishing on self-publishing platforms or selling via your own app/site
  • Creating a brand and content strategy to reach readers
You can enhance your offering with audio versions, worksheets, or bundled packages. With minimal investment, it allows passive income and personal branding opportunities. E-book writing is ideal for educators, writers, and experts who want to build a digital product business while helping learners access knowledge conveniently.
