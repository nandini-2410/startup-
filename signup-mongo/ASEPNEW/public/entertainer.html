Party Entertainer

Startup Cost: ₹15,000 - ₹70,000
A Party Entertainer startup focuses on delivering fun, laughter, and engaging activities for all types of celebrations — from kids' birthday parties to corporate gatherings. Your services can include:
  • Clown or character performances
  • Magic shows, puppet shows, or storytelling
  • Games coordination, face painting, or balloon twisting
  • Music, dance, or comedy acts for adults or families
With minimal investment in costumes, props, and sound equipment, entertainers can start solo or hire a small team. Strong communication skills, creativity, and the ability to engage a crowd are must-haves. Marketing through WhatsApp groups, local schools, event planners, and Instagram reels can attract bookings. It's a flexible business — you can perform on weekends or full-time and scale into a full entertainment company. Positive reviews, client trust, and memorable experiences will keep this business growing through word of mouth.
