Wedding Consultant

Startup Cost: ₹1,00,000 - ₹5,00,000
A Wedding Consultant startup focuses on helping couples plan their dream wedding stress-free. From budget planning to vendor coordination, wedding consultants ensure every detail is managed professionally and on time. Key services include:
  • Venue selection and theme planning
  • Coordination with caterers, decorators, photographers
  • Timeline management and guest coordination
  • Handling rituals, entertainment, and logistics
You can start small with local weddings, building a portfolio through personalized service. Networking with florists, makeup artists, and venues gives you a strong advantage. Software tools can be used for scheduling, reminders, and budget tracking. Market yourself via wedding expos, Instagram wedding reels, Google Business Profile, and word-of-mouth. The trust you build with clients will drive referrals and repeat business in families. Wedding planning is emotional and impactful — your role is not just logistics but creating memories. A reliable and creative consultant can grow into a full-fledged wedding planning agency.
