Online Course

Startup Cost: ₹15,000 - ₹1,00,000
Launching an online course allows educators to teach students globally without the need for a physical classroom. It’s scalable, flexible, and generates passive income once created. You can start by:
  • Choosing a niche (e.g., coding, finance, soft skills, design, etc.)
  • Recording lessons using screen capture tools or cameras
  • Uploading content to platforms like Udemy, Teachable, or your own site
  • Offering quizzes, assignments, and certificates
Initial investments go into course planning, video recording equipment, editing software, and optionally a website. Creating high-quality content with engaging visuals and clear explanations is crucial. Promotion via social media, SEO blogs, YouTube previews, and email marketing helps drive traffic and enrollments. This startup has great potential for long-term earnings and brand growth in the ed-tech space.
